Motorola MDC1200


About MDC1200

Many of our club members have Motorola commercial radios that use MDC1200. MDC1200 is a low-data rate system that allows for User ID, Selective Calling, Paging and Radio Check. IDs are transmitted at the beginning and end of transmission. We are looking into implementing status messages in the near future. Once we do another table will be created on this page containing them. IDs are based on the DTMF lettering of the last 4 characters of a callsign to create a semi-unique value. The Club fleet ID is 862.

Kenwood FleetSync


About Fleetsync

For the few members that run Kenwood equipment, our club also uses Fleetsync at 1200 baud. ID's based on the same system as our MDC1200 ID's. The same 4 digit ID's are used in both systems.
